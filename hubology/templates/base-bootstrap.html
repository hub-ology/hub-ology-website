About

hub-ology is a 501(c)(3) public charity dedicated to equiping, educating, and encouraging the best rural problem solvers so that they might do more with computing.

We'd love to have you attend one of our hub.py meet ups or support our PyTruck project to bring free equipment and training to a rural area near you

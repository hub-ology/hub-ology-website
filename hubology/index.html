About hub-ology

hub-ology is dedicated to equiping, educating, and encouraging rural problem solvers to do more with computing. The IRS classifies hub-ology as a public charity under section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. Our EIN is 46-3997463. A copy of our determination letter is available online.

Equipment

Most of the money we raise goes directly toward the purchase of computer hardware for rural problem solvers. This often includes low cost, single board computers like the Raspberry Pi and accessories.

hub.py

The Hub City Python User Group (hub.py) was started to help provide free meetups and training sessions related to the Python programming language and various topics in computer science. All meetings are free and open to the public. We'd love to have you join our meetup page and attend the meetups that are of interest to you.

PyTruck

The PyTruck is our flagship project to bring free computer hardware and training sessions to rural problem solvers. We realize that it is not always possible for some of the best problem solvers in rural or remote areas to attend meetups and training sessions that are common in cities these days. So we're going to bring free hardware and training to them. We're currently raising funds to complete the first PyTruck. Please consider making a donation so that we can use the PyTruck to provide more outreach to rural areas.
